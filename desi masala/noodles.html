Veg Noodles

Ingredients:

  • Noodles
  • Assorted vegetables (carrots, bell peppers, cabbage, etc.), sliced thinly
  • Soy sauce
  • Red chili powder
  • Salt
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Capsicum
  • Onion
  • Tomato ketchup
  • Chili sauce
  • Pepper

Instructions:

    Cook noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside. In a pan, heat oil. Add minced garlic and ginger, sauté until fragrant. Add sliced onions, cook until translucent. Add assorted vegetables, stir-fry until tender yet crisp. Toss in cooked noodles. Season with soy sauce, chili sauce, vinegar, salt, and pepper to taste. Stir well to combine. Serve hot.

Schezwan Noodles

Ingredients:

  • Noodles
  • Mixed vegetables (carrots, bell peppers, cabbage, etc.), sliced thinly
  • Soy sauce
  • Schezwan sauce
  • Salt
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Capsicum
  • Onion
  • Tomato ketchup
  • Chili sauce
  • Pepper

Instructions:

    Cook noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside. Heat oil in a pan. Add minced garlic and ginger. Sauté until fragrant. Add sliced onion and stir-fry until translucent. Add mixed vegetables and cook until tender yet crisp. Mix in schezwan sauce and soy sauce. Stir well. Add cooked noodles to the pan. Toss everything together until noodles are coated evenly with the sauce. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with chopped spring onions. Serve hot.
