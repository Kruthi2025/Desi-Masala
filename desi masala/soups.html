Soups

Tomato Soup

Ingredients:

  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Cream
  • Olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Sauté chopped onion and minced garlic in olive oil until softened.
  2. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they break down.
  3. Blend the mixture until smooth.
  4. Return to the pot, add vegetable broth, and simmer.
  5. Season with salt, pepper, and sugar (optional).
  6. Serve hot with garnishes like basil leaves, cream, or grated cheese.

Veg Soup

Ingredients:

  • Assorted vegetables
  • Vegetable broth or water
  • Olive oil or butter
  • Garlic
  • Herbs and spices

Instructions:

  1. Sauté assorted veggies in olive oil.
  2. Add vegetable broth or water and simmer until veggies are tender.
  3. Season with herbs, spices, salt, and pepper.
  4. Optionally, blend for a smoother consistency.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!

Veg and Sweet Corn Soup

Ingredients:

  • Sweet corn kernels
  • Assorted vegetables
  • Vegetable broth or water
  • Olive oil or butter
  • Garlic and Ginger
  • Soy sauce
  • Cornstarch
  • Salt and pepper
  • Spring onion

Instructions:

  1. Soak and cook the chickpeas until tender.
  2. In a pan, sauté onions, garlic, and ginger.
  3. Add tomatoes and spices, and cook until tomatoes are soft.
  4. Add the cooked chickpeas to the mixture and simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  5. Adjust seasoning and add water if needed.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with naan or rice.
